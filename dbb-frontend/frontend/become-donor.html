General Requirements

Age

17-65 years old

Weight

At least 50kg

Health

Good general health

Frequency

Wait 56 days between donations

ID Required

Valid photo identification

Screening

Pass health screening

Important Notes

Bring a valid photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)

List all medications you're currently taking

Avoid alcohol 24 hours before donation

Eat a healthy meal before donating

Drink plenty of water before and after

Donation Process

Complete forms and health questionnaire

5-10 min

10-15 min

Blood collection process

8-12 min

Recovery

Rest and refreshments

10-15 min
Total Time: About 1 Hour
Actual Donation: 8-12 Minutes

How to Prepare for Donation

24 Hours Before

  • Get 7-8 hours of sleep
  • Eat iron-rich foods
  • Stay hydrated
  • Avoid alcohol

Day of Donation

  • Eat healthy meal
  • Drink extra water
  • Bring valid ID
  • Wear comfortable clothes

After Donation

  • Rest 10-15 minutes
  • Drink plenty of fluids
  • Avoid heavy lifting
  • Keep bandage on 4-5 hours

Preparation Tips

Eat Well

Good meal before donating. Avoid fatty foods.

Stay Hydrated

Drink water before and after donation.

Get Rest

Good sleep ensures best condition.

Know Medications

List all current medications.

Common Myths About Blood Donation

Myth: It's Painful

Fact: Brief pinch, then painless

Myth: Makes You Weak

Fact: Body quickly replaces blood

Myth: You Can Get Sick

Fact: Sterile equipment, no infection risk

Myth: Takes Too Long

Fact: 8-12 min donation, 1 hour total
