FAQ

How to register as donor?

Click "Become a Donor", fill the form, complete health questionnaire.

How often can I donate?

Every 56 days (8 weeks) for whole blood. System tracks eligibility automatically.

Is my data secure?

Yes, encrypted storage, HIPAA compliant, never shared without consent.

Emergency requests?

Instant notifications to eligible donors via SMS, email, push notifications.
